Overview

We have developed a specialized Large Language Model (LLM) dedicated to bamboo botany. By deep learning from tens of thousands of samples, it combines image analysis with botanical text to provide expert-level identification and interpretation of subtle morphological features, bridging the gap between plant science and artificial intelligence.
